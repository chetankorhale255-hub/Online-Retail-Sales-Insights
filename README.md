# 📊 Online Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## Overview

This project is an end-to-end *sales data analysis* performed using *Microsoft Excel* on an online retail dataset. The objective was to clean raw transactional data, build analytical reports, create an interactive dashboard, and generate business insights that can support decision-making.

The project demonstrates practical Excel data analysis techniques including *Power Query, **Pivot Tables, **Pivot Charts, **Slicers, **Timeline Filters*, dashboard design, and business insight reporting.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this project is to:

- Analyze historical online retail sales performance.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Identify high-performing products and markets.
- Compare domestic and international sales.
- Discover seasonal sales trends.
- Present findings through an interactive Excel dashboard.

---

# 📁 Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional records from an online retail business, including information such as:

- Invoice Number
- Invoice Date
- Customer ID
- Country
- Product Description
- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Revenue

The data was cleaned and transformed before analysis to improve reporting accuracy.

# 📌 Key Insights

- The business generated approximately *$10.6 million* in revenue across *19,968 invoices*.
- Sales are highly concentrated in the domestic market, accounting for *90%* of total revenue.
- The business serves customers across *38 countries*, demonstrating an international presence despite the majority of revenue coming from domestic sales.
- Sales activity increases significantly between *September and November*, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- A relatively small number of products contribute a substantial share of overall revenue, with the top three products accounting for *47%* of sales.
- Interactive filters enable users to analyze sales by time period and other available dimensions for flexible reporting.

> *Note:* The dataset includes records for *December 2011, which may be incomplete. Therefore, any observed decline in sales during December should **not* be interpreted as a confirmed business trend without considering the partial nature of the available data.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Focus inventory planning and marketing campaigns ahead of the September–November peak sales period.
- Continue monitoring high-performing products to maintain availability during periods of increased demand.
- Explore opportunities to increase international sales, which currently represent only *10%* of total revenue.
- Review product concentration risk by expanding sales across a broader range of products.
- Use the dashboard regularly to monitor sales performance and support data-driven decision-making.

---## 📌 Overall Conclusion

The business demonstrates *strong overall performance, generating *$10.6M in total revenue*, processing **19,960 invoices, and serving customers across **38 countries*.

Key findings from the analysis include:

- 💰 *Total Revenue:* *$10.6M*
- 🧾 *Total Invoices:* *19,960*
- 🌍 *Geographical Presence:* *38 countries*

### 📈 Sales Performance

- Sales increase significantly during the *September to November* period, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- *November* is the highest-performing month in terms of revenue.
- Although *December* shows a sharp decline in sales, this should *not* be interpreted as a business downturn without verifying whether the December dataset is complete. A partial month's data can naturally result in lower revenue.

### 🌍 Market Insights

- The business is *highly dependent on the UK market*, which contributes the majority of revenue.
- This creates a *market concentration risk*, making the business vulnerable to changes in demand within a single region.

### 💡 Business Recommendations

- Expand into *international markets* to reduce dependency on the UK.
- Diversify the *product catalog* to attract a broader customer base.
- Improve *inventory planning* by preparing for the high-demand season between *September and November*.

### 📊 Final Insight

The business generated *$10.6M in total revenue*, and the monthly sales trend explains how this revenue accumulated throughout the year. Overall, the company shows healthy growth and strong seasonal performance, while future success will depend on expanding globally, reducing market concentration risk, and maintaining efficient inventory management.
